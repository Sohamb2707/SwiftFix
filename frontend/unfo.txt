SwiftFix is a website(Platform) developed to help individuals to get all the home services under one roof i.e a user friendly home service website. Getting home services 
at affordable market prices and instantly is quite a tedious task our appication aims to ease this task by providing a one stop solution. SwiftFix is useful for both the one
whoe are looking for home services and the one who have skills but are unable to find a job. SwiftFix has various features with user-friendly home-services display
like a chatbot to answer your questions all the time as per your need, multilingual support(english & hindi both), subscription based model, dark-light mode
functionality, cheaper packages if you get multiple home services and many more. SwiftFix ensures the background checks of the home service providers(SwiftStars)
to ensure a peace of mind for individuals who book home services. During festive season like navratre and diwali we have a discount offer going on with 20% off on 
the total amount if you have DISCOUNT20 promocode. Also we take care of our SwiftStars(Home service providers) by providing top rates for their skills, 
consistent job availability, adaptable schedules, etc
